# LeadGen Performance Analytics

An end-to-end **Business Development performance analytics** case study based on a real Data Analyst skills assessment.

## Objective
Analyze lead generation data, answer business-driven questions, and provide actionable insights using dashboards and predictive analysis.
This project was completed as part of a **Data Analyst skills assessment** asked by recruiter from Antarctica Global - The Windows

## Business Questions Answered
- Who are the top-performing associates based on total leads?
- Who is the most efficient associate (leads per minute)?
- Who is the most consistent performer across days?
- How does time spent on lead generation impact output?
- Is there an optimal time window for maximum productivity?
- Do team reviews impact lead generation performance?
- Is there a difference between weekday and weekend performance?
- What patterns exist on high-performing (top 10%) days?
- Are incomplete leads reducing over time?
- Can leads be predicted based on time spent?
